# Supply Agreement — AlpenHX GmbH

**Reference:** AH-2022-021
**Supplier ID:** SUP-004
**Parties:** AlpenHX GmbH ("Supplier") and the Manufacturer ("Buyer")
**Category:** Brazed-plate heat exchangers

## 1. Term and Renewal

This Agreement commences on 1 September 2022 for an initial term of three (3) years, renewing for successive twelve (12) month periods unless either party gives ninety (90) days written notice before expiry.

## 2. Force Majeure

A party is relieved of liability to the extent performance is prevented by acts of God, war, terrorism, government action, or disruption to transport and logistics. The affected party must give written notice within ten (10) days of the event and shall use reasonable efforts to mitigate.

## 3. Delivery and Lead Time

Standard replenishment lead time is nine (9) weeks from a confirmed purchase order.

## 4. Minimum Order Quantity

The Buyer commits to a minimum annual offtake equal to seventy percent (70%) of the prior year's purchased volume.

## 5. Alternative Sourcing

The Buyer may qualify and purchase equivalent heat exchangers from an alternative supplier on thirty (30) days written notice to the Supplier.

## 6. Price and Adjustment

Prices are fixed annually and reviewed at renewal.

## 7. Quality SLA and Liability

The Supplier's aggregate liability is capped at the value of the affected purchase order. Liquidated damages of zero point four percent (0.4%) of the affected purchase order value are payable per week of delay, capped at eight percent (8%).

## 8. Termination

Either party may terminate for material breach uncured sixty (60) days after written notice.

## 9. Notice and Governance

All notices shall be in writing. This Agreement is governed by the laws of Germany. Disputes shall be referred to arbitration seated in Frankfurt.
