# Supply Agreement — Zhejiang Scroll Tech Co

**Reference:** ZS-2023-007
**Supplier ID:** SUP-002
**Parties:** Zhejiang Scroll Tech Co ("Supplier") and the Manufacturer ("Buyer")
**Category:** Scroll compressors for the mid-range chiller line

## 1. Term and Renewal

This Agreement commences on 1 March 2023 for an initial term of two (2) years, renewing for successive twelve (12) month periods unless either party gives ninety (90) days written notice before expiry.

## 2. Force Majeure

A party is relieved of liability to the extent performance is prevented by an event beyond its reasonable control, including acts of God, war, government action, and export-control or licensing action by a competent authority. The affected party must give written notice within ten (10) days of the event.

## 3. Delivery and Lead Time

Standard replenishment lead time is ten (10) weeks from a confirmed purchase order.

## 4. Minimum Order Quantity

There is no minimum annual offtake commitment under this Agreement.

## 5. Alternative Sourcing

The Buyer expressly retains the right to qualify and purchase scroll compressors of equivalent specification from one or more additional suppliers at its sole discretion. This Agreement is non-exclusive and creates no sole-source obligation on the Buyer.

## 6. Price and Adjustment

Prices are fixed for each twelve (12) month period and reviewed at renewal.

## 7. Quality SLA and Liability

The Supplier's aggregate liability is capped at the value of the affected purchase order. Liquidated damages of zero point three percent (0.3%) of the affected purchase order value are payable per week of delay, capped at eight percent (8%).

## 8. Termination

The Buyer may terminate for convenience on ninety (90) days written notice. Either party may terminate for material breach uncured sixty (60) days after written notice.

## 9. Notice and Governance

All notices shall be in writing. This Agreement is governed by the laws of Singapore, as a neutral seat. Disputes shall be referred to arbitration in Singapore.
