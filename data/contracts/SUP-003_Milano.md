# Supply Agreement — Milano Controls Srl

**Reference:** MC-2022-014
**Supplier ID:** SUP-003
**Parties:** Milano Controls Srl ("Supplier") and the Manufacturer ("Buyer")
**Category:** Building management system controllers and premium controller firmware

## 1. Term and Renewal

This Agreement commences on 1 July 2022 for an initial term of three (3) years, renewing for successive twelve (12) month periods unless either party gives ninety (90) days written notice before expiry.

## 2. Force Majeure

A party is relieved of liability to the extent performance is prevented by any event beyond that party's reasonable control. Without limitation, such events include acts of God, war, terrorism, government action, supplier plant failure, interruption of energy supply, supply-chain disruption, and delay or unavailability of transport. The affected party must give written notice within fifteen (15) days and shall use all reasonable efforts to mitigate the effect of the event and resume performance as soon as practicable.

## 3. Delivery and Lead Time

Standard replenishment lead time is twelve (12) weeks from a confirmed purchase order.

## 4. Minimum Order Quantity

The Buyer commits to a minimum annual offtake equal to seventy-five percent (75%) of the prior year's purchased volume.

## 5. Alternative Sourcing

The Buyer may qualify and purchase a secondary source for non-proprietary components on written notice to the Supplier. Proprietary controller firmware and the premium controller models incorporating it remain sole-source to the Supplier for the term of this Agreement.

## 6. Business Continuity

The Supplier shall maintain a documented Business Continuity Plan addressing component sourcing, alternative production capacity, and disaster recovery. The Supplier shall provide the current Business Continuity Plan to the Buyer on request and shall review it with the Buyer annually. Failure to maintain a current Business Continuity Plan is a material breach of this Agreement.

## 7. Quality SLA and Liability

The Supplier's aggregate liability is capped at two (2) times the value of the affected purchase order. Liquidated damages of zero point five percent (0.5%) of the affected purchase order value are payable per week of delay, capped at ten percent (10%).

## 8. Termination

Either party may terminate for material breach that remains uncured forty-five (45) days after written notice.

## 9. Notice and Governance

All notices shall be in writing. This Agreement is governed by the laws of Italy. Disputes shall be referred to arbitration seated in Milan.
