# Supply Agreement — Rheinkomp Compressors GmbH

**Reference:** RK-2023-001
**Supplier ID:** SUP-001
**Parties:** Rheinkomp Compressors GmbH ("Supplier") and the Manufacturer ("Buyer")
**Category:** Screw compressors for the flagship district-cooling chiller line

## 1. Term and Renewal

This Agreement commences on 1 January 2023 for an initial term of three (3) years. It renews automatically for successive twelve (12) month periods unless either party gives one hundred and eighty (180) days written notice before the then-current term expires.

## 2. Force Majeure

A party is relieved of liability for failure to perform only to the extent that performance is prevented by an event of force majeure. For the purposes of this Agreement, force majeure means solely the following events: (a) acts of God, being earthquake, flood, or natural catastrophe; (b) war, armed conflict, terrorism, or civil insurrection; and (c) embargo, sanction, or prohibition imposed by a competent government authority.

Force majeure expressly excludes, and shall not extend to, any of the following events: failure, breakdown, or shutdown of the Supplier's manufacturing plant or equipment; shortage, interruption, or increase in the cost of energy, electricity, gas, or fuel; shortage or increase in the cost of raw materials or components; labour disputes, strikes, or industrial action involving the Supplier's own workforce; and delay, congestion, or unavailability of shipping, freight, or transport.

A party claiming force majeure must give written notice within five (5) business days of the event. Failure to give notice within this period forfeits all relief under this clause.

## 3. Delivery and Lead Time

Standard replenishment lead time is sixteen (16) weeks from the date of a confirmed purchase order. The Supplier shall use commercially reasonable efforts to meet confirmed delivery dates.

## 4. Minimum Order Quantity

The Buyer commits to a minimum annual offtake equal to eighty percent (80%) of the prior year's purchased volume.

## 5. Alternative Sourcing

During the term of this Agreement, the Buyer shall not qualify, source, or purchase compressors of the contracted specifications from any third party without the Supplier's prior written consent. The Supplier is appointed sole supplier of the contracted compressor models for the duration of this Agreement.

## 6. Price and Adjustment

Prices are fixed annually. The Supplier may pass through documented raw-material index movements above eight percent (8%) on sixty (60) days written notice.

## 7. Quality SLA and Liability

Defect liability is limited to repair or replacement of the affected goods. The Supplier's aggregate liability under this Agreement is capped at the value of the affected purchase order. No liquidated damages are payable for late or non-delivery.

## 8. Termination

Either party may terminate for material breach that remains uncured sixty (60) days after written notice. The Buyer has no right of termination for convenience during the term.

## 9. Notice and Governance

All notices shall be in writing. This Agreement is governed by the laws of Germany. Disputes shall be referred to arbitration seated in Frankfurt.
